### ✅ DOs
You may use the media kit in the following ways:
- Talk about Katorly Lab.
- Link to Katorly Lab.
- Link to our code and content.
- Use them in a blog post or news article related to Katorly Lab.
- Use them in pictures or videos related to Katorly Lab.

### ❌ DON'Ts
Please DO NOT do the following:
- Modify them or integrate parts of them into your own work.
- Use them as the icon or logo for your business/organization, offering, project, domain name, social media account, or website.
- Stretch, recolor, outline, or rotate the logo.
- Use our branding or marks on merchandise.

*If you still have questions about using them, feel free to open a discussion [here](https://github.com/orgs/katorlys/discussions).*
